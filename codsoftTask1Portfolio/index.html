About Me

I am Diane INGABIRE,

Growing up in a rural community, agriculture was central to life. I admired the dedication of local farmers but saw their struggles with outdated methods and unpredictable challenges. This inspired me to study agricultural sciences, learning about sustainable farming and modern technologies. Returning home, I organized workshops to share my knowledge. Introducing advanced techniques improved productivity and sustainability for local farmers. My mission is to continue advocating for agricultural progress, empowering farmers with the latest tools and knowledge. The resilience of the farmers from my childhood motivates me to drive positive change in agriculture.
